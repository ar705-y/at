Snap History Metadata

This section includes metadata information about Snaps you have recently sent and received, including the timestamp when it was sent or received and the type of media shared (video, image, media, etc.), which is represented by an icon. If you have saved a Snap in Chat, it will be available in the Chat Media folder of your data download, if you elected to include your Memories and other saved media in your download.
